Kalikattu Ithikasam

Book Details

Title: Kallikattu Ithikasam (கள்ளிக்காட்டு இதிகாசம்)

Author: Vairamuthu

Language: Tamil

Publisher: Thirumagal Nilaiyam

Publication Date: January 1, 2019

Plot Summary

A deeply moving story about the struggles of farmers in the riverbed regions of Tamil Nadu. This novel masterfully weaves emotions of loss, hardship, and resilience. Rooted in real-life experiences, it captures the raw and powerful essence of rural life.
